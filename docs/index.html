Typing Time Attack

Liem Vien

Contact: liemahvien@gmail.com

This game is a typing tutorial, with randomized sentence generators to add randomness (and some ridiculousness). Choose from 5 difficulty options, and type away at the screen for 1 minute. Type the prompt quickly before it hits the bottom of the window! You can improve your typing skills, and keep track of your progress by looking at the HUD. The HUD displays your score, along with your Words-per-minute(WPM),Accuracy, and time left. Also see if you can find a hidden easter egg!
